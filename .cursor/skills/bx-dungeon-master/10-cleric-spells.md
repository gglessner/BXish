# Cleric Spells

### L1
**Cure Light Wounds** (R:touch): Heal 1d6+1 OR cure paralysis. Rev: Cause — 1d6+1 dmg, atk roll.
**Detect Evil** (R:120', D:6T): Evil intent/objects glow.
**Detect Magic** (R:60', D:2T): Enchanted things glow.
**Light** (R:120', D:12T): 15' light; blind (sv). Rev: Darkness — 15' black, not infra.
**Prot from Evil** (R:self, D:12T): +1 sv, foes –1 atk. Summoned can't melee; breaks if caster attacks.
**Purify Food & Water** (R:10'): Purify food/drink for 12.
**Remove Fear** (R:touch, D:2T): Cure fear (+1/lvl vs magic). Rev: Cause Fear — flee, sv neg.
**Resist Cold** (R:30', D:6T): Immune non-magic cold, +2 sv magic cold, –1 dmg/die.

### L2
**Bless** (R:60', D:6T): 20' allies not in melee +1 atk/dmg/morale. Rev: Blight — –1, sv neg.
**Find Traps** (R:30', D:2T): Traps glow blue.
**Hold Person** (R:180', D:9T): Paralyse humans, sv neg. Single –2. Group 1d4. No undead/>4+1HD.
**Know Alignment** (R:10', D:1rd): One target's alignment.
**Resist Fire** (R:30', D:2T): One creature immune non-magic fire, +2 sv magic fire, –1 dmg/die.
**Silence 15'R** (R:180', D:12T): 15' silence, no spells. On creature: sv or moves with.
**Snake Charm** (R:60', D:1d4+1): Snakes non-hostile, total HD ≤ caster lvl.
**Speak w/ Animals** (R:30', D:6T): Talk to one animal type.

### L3
**Continual Light** (R:120', D:perm): 30' daylight; blind (sv). Rev: C.Darkness — blocks sight+infra.
**Cure Disease** (R:30'): Cure any disease; kill green slime. Rev: Cause Disease — sv or die 2d12 days.
**Growth of Animal** (R:120', D:12T): One animal 2× size/dmg/carry.
**Locate Object** (R:120', D:6T): Direction to object type or specific known. Not creatures.
**Remove Curse** (R:touch): Remove one curse; discard cursed item. Rev: Curse — sv or –2sv/–4hit/50%ability.
**Striking** (R:30', D:1T): Weapon +1d6 dmg, counts magical.

### L4
**Create Water** (R:touch): ~50 gal (12 humans+mounts/day). +12/lvl>8.
**Cure Serious Wounds** (R:touch): Heal 2d6+2. Rev: Cause — 2d6+2 dmg.
**Neutralize Poison** (R:touch): Cure poison; revive poison death within 10 rds.
**Prot Evil 10'R** (R:10' around, D:12T): As Prot Evil for all in 10'. Breaks if warded melees.
**Speak w/ Plants** (R:30', D:3T): Talk to plants/plant monsters.
**Sticks to Snakes** (R:120', D:6T): 2d8 sticks→snakes (50% poison). AC6 HD1 1d4 MV90'.

### L5
**Commune** (R:self, D:3T): 3 yes/no to deity (6 once/yr). Max 1/wk.
**Create Food** (R:self): Food for 12 humans+mounts. +12/lvl>8.
**Dispel Evil** (R:30', D:conc): Ward (enchanted/undead sv or banished) OR single –2sv or banished OR free cursed.
**Insect Plague** (R:480', D:conc): Outdoors. 60' swarm. ≤2HD flee. Ends if caster moves.
**Quest** (R:30'): Command task (not suicidal), sv neg. Refusal=curse. Rev: Remove Quest.
**Raise Dead** (R:120'): Restore dead ≤4 days/lvl>7. 1hp, 2wk recovery. OR destroy 1 undead (sv). Rev: Finger of Death — sv or die.
