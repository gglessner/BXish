# Strongholds
## Construction
1. Permission from local authority (not needed in wilderness)
2. Clear monsters in 6mi area
3. Design + calculate costs
4. 1 engineer per 100kgp cost
5. Build: 1 day per 500gp total
6. Clear wider area for domain
In towns: stone=40%, wood=20% of listed price.
## Domain
Patrol: 18mi max (6mi swamp/mountain/jungle). Hire mercs for patrol.
Settlers attracted by safety. Tax: 10gp/settler/year.
## Structure Prices
Barbican 37,000gp, Bastion 9,000, Civilian stone 3,000, Civilian wood 1,500, Drawbridge 500, Gatehouse 6,500, Keep 75,000, Moat 400, Subterranean passage 500, Tower lg 30,000, Tower sm 15,000, Castle wall 5,000
## Non-Standard Dimensions
Castle walls: 100' of 30'h=7500gp, each +10'h(max 60')=+5000gp.
Towers: max h=2× base dia. ≤base h: 200gp×base/10'. >base h: 400gp×base/10'. Bastions: tower÷2.
## Interior
Simple: +25% total covers doors, stairs, floors, roof, windows, bars, furnishings.
**Detailed:** Arrow slit 10, Door wood 10/reinforced 20/iron 50/stone 50, Floor flagstone 100/wood 40(10'×10'), Roof tile 100/wood 40, Secret door 5× cost, Shifting wall 1000, Stairs stone 60/wood 20(3'×10'), Trapdoor wood 20/reinforced 40/iron 120/stone 120, Window 10, Bars 10, Shutters 5
