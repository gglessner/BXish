# Monster Rules
## Stat Format
AC X[Y], HD Z(Nhp), Att attacks(dmg), THAC0 X[+Y], MV speed, SV class(lvl), ML X, AL, XP, NA dungeon(wilderness), TT type.
HD *=special ability(XP calc). ½HD=1d4. MV: base(encounter=⅓). ML: 2d6>ML=flee. NA: 2nd val=lair; wilderness lair=×5. TT: A-O=hoard/lair, P-T=individual, U-V=group.
## Saving Throws by HD
NH:14/15/16/17/18. 1-3:12/13/14/15/16. 4-6:10/11/12/13/14. 7-9:8/9/10/10/12. 10-12:6/7/8/8/10. 13-15:4/5/6/5/8. 16-18:2/3/4/3/6. 19-21:2/2/2/2/4. 22+:2/2/2/2/2. (D/W/P/B/S)
## THAC0 by HD
NH=20[-1], ≤1=19[0], 1+-2=18[+1], 2+-3=17[+2], 3+-4=16[+3], 4+-5=15[+4], 5+-6=14[+5], 6+-7=13[+6], 7+-9=12[+7], 9+-11=11[+8], 11+-13=10[+9], 13+-15=9[+10], 15+-17=8[+11], 17+-19=7[+12], 19+-21=6[+13], 21+=5[+14].
## XP by HD (Base+Bonus)
<1=5+1, 1=10+3, 1+=15+4, 2=20+5, 2+=25+10, 3=35+15, 3+=50+25, 4=75+50, 4+=125+75, 5=175+125, 5+=225+175, 6=275+225, 6+=350+300, 7-7+=450+400, 8-8+=650+550, 9-10+=900+700, 11-12+=1100+800, 13-16+=1350+950, 17-20+=2000+1150, 21+=2500+2000. >21:+250 both/HD.
## Treasure Types (Hoard)
A=18kgp avg. B=2k. C=1k. D=3.9k. E=2.3k. F=7.7k. G=23k. H=60k. I=11k. J=25gp. K=180gp. L=240gp. M=50k. N=potions only. O=scrolls only.
Individual: P=0.1gp, Q=1gp, R=3gp, S=5gp, T=17gp. Group: U=160gp, V=330gp.
## General
All non-humans have infravision 60' unless noted. 20% intelligent monsters speak Common.
**Persons** (charm person etc): Acolyte, Bandit, Berserker, Brigand, Buccaneer, Bugbear, Dervish, Dwarf, Elf, Gnoll, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Hobgoblin, Kobold, Lizard Man, Medium, Merchant, Merman, Neanderthal, Nixie, Noble, Nomad, Normal Human, Ogre, Orc, Pirate, Pixie, Sprite, Trader, Troglodyte, Veteran.
