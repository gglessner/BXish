# MU Spells 4–6

### L4
**Charm Monster** (R:120', D:indef): As Charm Person but 3d6 ≤3HD or 1 >3HD; any non-undead. Same sv table.
**Confusion** (R:120', D:12rds): 3d6 targets in 60'. ≤2HD no sv, ≥2+1HD sv/rd.
2d6: 2–5 atk caster's group, 6–8 no action, 9–12 atk own group.
**Dimension Door** (R:10'): Teleport self/1 creature ≤360' to known spot. Solid=fail. Unwilling: sv.
**Growth of Plants** (R:120', D:perm): 3,000 sq ft→impassable thorns.
**Hallucinatory Terrain** (R:240', D:until touched): Create/hide terrain. Touch negates.
**Massmorph** (R:240', D:perm): Creatures in 240' dia appear as trees. Leaving ends.
**Polymorph Others** (R:60', D:perm): Transform creature (new HD ≤2× subject). Becomes new form+behaviour. Same hp. Sv neg. Reverts on death.
**Polymorph Self** (R:self, D:6T+1/lvl): Become form HD ≤ lvl. Keep INT/hp/sv/atks. Gain physical. Can't cast.
**Remove Curse** (R:touch): Remove one curse. Rev: Curse — sv or –2sv/–4hit/50%ability.
**Wall of Fire** (R:60', D:conc): Opaque fire ≤1200sqft. <4HD can't pass. ≥4HD: 1d6 (2× undead/cold).
**Wall of Ice** (R:120', D:12T): Ice ≤1200sqft on solid. <4HD can't pass. ≥4HD: 1d6 (2× fire).
**Wizard Eye** (R:240', D:6T): Invisible eye, 120'/turn, infravision. Through 1" holes.

### L5
**Animate Dead** (R:60', D:perm): HD=caster lvl as skeletons (AC7) or zombies (AC8, HD+1).
**Cloudkill** (R:30', D:6T): 30' poison fog, drifts 60'/T, sinks. 1hp/rd. <5HD sv vs death/rd or die.
**Conjure Elemental** (R:240', D:perm): 16HD (AC–2, 72hp, 3d8). Needs element source. Conc to control; break=attacks caster. 1/plane/day.
**Contact Higher Plane** (R:self): Yes/no Qs, 1/wk. Insanity –5%/lvl>11. Recovery=plane# wks.
Plane(#Q/DK%/Truth%/Ins%): 3(3/75/50/5) 4(4/70/55/10) 5(5/65/60/15) 6(6/60/65/20) 7(7/50/70/25) 8(8/40/75/30) 9(9/30/80/35) 10(10/20/85/40) 11(11/10/90/45) 12(12/5/95/50).
**Feeblemind** (R:240', D:perm): Arcane caster sv –4 or can't think/cast.
**Hold Monster** (R:120', D:6T+1/lvl): Paralyse creatures, sv. Single –2, group 1d4. Not undead.
**Magic Jar** (R:self): Soul→object 30'; body comatose. Possess in 120' (sv neg, retry 1T). Full control, can't cast. Jar destroyed=die. Body destroyed=stranded.
**Pass-Wall** (R:30', D:3T): 5' hole through ≤10' rock/stone.
**Telekinesis** (R:120', D:conc/6rds): Move 200cn/lvl at 20'/rd. Creature: sv.
**Teleport** (R:10'): Self/1 creature anywhere with gear. Must know destination. Unwilling: sv.
Scant(01–50 ok/51–75 high/76–00 low) Moderate(01–80/81–90/91–00) Exact(01–95/96–99/00). High=1d10×10' up (solid=dead, else fall). Low=ground, dead.
**Transmute Rock to Mud** (R:120', D:3d6 days): 3,000sqft rock→10' mud; 90% mv penalty. Rev: Mud to Rock — perm.
**Wall of Stone** (R:60', D:perm): 1,000 cu ft rock wall on solid surface.

### L6
**Anti-Magic Shell** (R:self, D:12T): No spells/effects in or out. Dismiss OK.
**Control Weather** (R:240yds, D:conc): Outdoors, no move. Calm, heat(½mv), fog(20'vis/½mv), wind(½mv/no missiles), rain(–2 missiles/mud), snow(20'vis/½mv), tornado(2d8/360'fly/<2HD swept).
**Death Spell** (R:240'): 4d8 HD in 60' cube sv vs death or die. Not undead/>7HD.
**Disintegrate** (R:60'): Destroy 1 non-magic creature (sv vs death) or object.
**Geas** (R:30', D:perm): Command task, sv neg. Non-compliance→death. Rev: Remove Geas — 5% fail/lvl diff.
**Invisible Stalker** (R:self): Summon stalker (AC3 HD8 4d4 MV120'). Tracks perfectly. Surprise 1–5. Twists long orders.
**Lower Water** (R:240', D:10T): Halve water depth ≤10,000sqft.
**Move Earth** (R:240', D:6T): Reshape earth (not stone) at 60'/turn.
**Part Water** (R:120', D:6T): 10'×120' path through water.
**Projected Image** (R:240', D:6T): Illusory double; spells from it. Immune spells/missiles. Melee dispels.
**Reincarnation** (R:self, D:perm): Dead returns new form. Class lvl 1d6 (max former). Monster HD ≤ former lvl (max 6).
