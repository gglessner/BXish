# Combat
## Round Sequence
1)Declare spells+melee movement 2)Initiative 1d6/side 3)Winner acts: a)Morale b)Movement c)Missile d)Spells e)Melee 4)Other sides act same order.
## Initiative
1d6/side, highest first. Ties: reroll or simultaneous. Slow weapons(2h melee): always last. Optional individual: per person+DEX mod.
## Movement
Outside melee: ⅓ base/round (max 60rds). Fighting withdrawal: ½ encounter rate, can attack. Retreat: full rate, can't attack, opponent +2 att ignores shield.
## Missile
Requires >5' dist. Short +1, Medium 0, Long -1, Beyond=impossible. Cover: complete=untargetable, partial -1 to -4.
## Spells
Speak+hands required. Sole action, no MV. Needs line of sight. Disruption: caster loses init & hit/fails save before acting→spell lost.
## Attack Rolls
1d20+mods (STR melee; DEX+range+cover missile). Hit if rolled AC≤target AC→roll dmg. Nat20=always hit, nat1=always miss. Ascending AC: 1d20+mods+bonus≥target AAC. THAC0 shortcut: THAC0-targetAC=needed roll.
## Damage
PCs: 1d6+STR(melee). Monsters: per description. Min 1hp on hit. 0hp=dead. Variable weapon dmg optional.
## Other Rules
- Behind: negate shield AC
- Blind: can't attack
- Bombing(flying): max 300', hit 16+
- Paralysed: auto-hit, roll dmg
- Spacing: 10' passage=2-3 side-by-side
- Subduing: bludgeon only, track separately, 0hp=surrender
- Unarmed: 1d2+STR
- Unstable(ships/mounts): missile -4, no spells, magic items OK
- Water combat: att/dmg penalised, missiles usually fail underwater
## Morale
Rating 2-12. 2d6>ML=flee/surrender. Check: first ally killed, half incapacitated. Two successes=fights to death. ±2 situational. ML2=never fights, ML12=never checks.
Mercenary ML: Militia 6, Barbarian 7, Trained 8, Mounted +1, Elite +1, Fanatic +2. Retainers use loyalty checks.
## Monster Saving Throws
NH: D14/W15/P16/B17/S18. 1-3: 12/13/14/15/16. 4-6: 10/11/12/13/14. 7-9: 8/9/10/10/12. 10-12: 6/7/8/8/10. 13-15: 4/5/6/5/8. 16-18: 2/3/4/3/6. 19-21: 2/2/2/2/4. 22+: 2/2/2/2/2.
## Attack Matrix (THAC0 by HD)
NH=20[-1], ≤1=19[0], 1+-2=18[+1], 2+-3=17[+2], 3+-4=16[+3], 4+-5=15[+4], 5+-6=14[+5], 6+-7=13[+6], 7+-9=12[+7], 9+-11=11[+8], 11+-13=10[+9], 13+-15=9[+10], 15+-17=8[+11], 17+-19=7[+12], 19+-21=6[+13], 21+=5[+14].
Bonus HP monsters (e.g. 2+1): attack as 1 HD higher. PCs use class THAC0. Normal humans=NH.
