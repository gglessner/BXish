# Vehicles & Mounts
## Vehicle Rules
HP=hull points, 0=destroyed (ships sink 1d10 rnd). Per 10% hp lost=10% speed lost. Normal weapons=no hull dmg. Magic/giants=1hp/5dmg. Mounted weapons=direct hull dmg. Repair: workshop=full, field=half, 5crew=1hp/turn. Boarding: forced 2-in-6, mutual auto, boarders −2 atk/AC 1 rnd.
