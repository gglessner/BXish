# Adventure Design
## Dungeon Design
Setting(d6): 1=Crypt 2=Caverns 3=Stronghold 4=Mine 5=Temple 6=Tower. Pick monsters matching setting. Map 10'/sq. Key important areas first. Deeper=greater danger+reward; level N≈N HD monsters.
## Random Room Stocking (d6)
1-2=Empty(treasure 1-in-6). 3-4=Monster(treasure 3-in-6). 5=Special(no treasure). 6=Trap(treasure 2-in-6).
Monster treasure: roll TT. No monster: use treasure-by-level table.
## Room Traps (d6)
1=Falling block 1d10(save P). 2=Gas(save poison or die). 3=Mist(harmless). 4=Pit(fall dmg). 5=Scything blade 1d8. 6=Slide to lower level.
## Treasure Traps (d6)
1=Darts 1d6×1d4. 2=Flash blind 1d8 turns(save S). 3=Hidden monster. 4=Illusion fake monster AC9[10],vanish if hit;"killed" PC uncon 1d4 turns. 5=Spray double wandering 1d6hrs. 6=Needle(save poison or die).
## Specials (d8)
1=Alarm. 2=Animating objects. 3=Falling block(blocks passage). 4=Illusory passages. 5=Shifting architecture. 6=Strange waters. 7=Teleporter. 8=Trapdoor.
## Treasure by Dungeon Level
Lvl1: 1d6×100sp; 50%:1d6×10gp; 5%:1d6 gems; 2%:1d6 jewellery; 2%:1 magic.
Lvl2-3: 1d12×100sp; 50%:1d6×100gp; 10%:1d6 gems; 5%:1d6 jewellery; 8%:1 magic.
Lvl4-5: 1d6×1000sp; 1d6×200gp; 20%:1d6 gems; 10%:1d6 jewellery; 10%:1 magic.
Lvl6-7: 1d6×2000sp; 1d6×500gp; 30%:1d6 gems; 15%:1d6 jewellery; 15%:1 magic.
Lvl8-9: 1d6×5000sp; 1d6×1000gp; 40%:1d12 gems; 20%:1d12 jewellery; 20%:1 magic.
## Wilderness Design
Map hex 24mi(large)/6mi(detail). Mark terrain, human/non-human realms, base town near river/road, dungeons ~1 day away.
## Base Town
Village 50-999. Small Town 1k-5k. Large Town 5k-15k. City 15k+. Services: black market, guards, healing, lodging, rumours.
## XP Awards
Treasure: 1XP/1gp(non-magic); typically ¾+ of total XP. Magic items: no XP.
**XP by HD**: <1=5+1, 1=10+3, 1+=15+4, 2=20+5, 2+=25+10, 3=35+15, 3+=50+25, 4=75+50, 4+=125+75, 5=175+125, 5+=225+175, 6=275+225, 6+=350+300, 7-7+=450+400, 8-8+=650+550, 9-10+=900+700, 11-12+=1100+800, 13-16+=1350+950, 17-20+=2000+1150, 21-21+=2500+2000. Each HD>21: +250 both.
Divide total XP evenly among all survivors including retainers.
