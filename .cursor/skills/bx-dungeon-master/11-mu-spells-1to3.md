# MU Spells 1–3

### L1
**Charm Person** (R:120', D:indef): Human/demihuman sv or charmed (obeys, no suicidal). Undead/>4+1HD immune.
Re-save by INT: 3–8 monthly, 9–12 weekly, 13–18 daily.
**Detect Magic** (R:60', D:2T): Enchanted things glow.
**Floating Disc** (R:6', D:6T): 3' disc carries 500 lbs, follows caster.
**Hold Portal** (R:10', D:2d6T): Hold door shut. Knock opens. 3+HD over caster forces.
**Light** (R:120', D:6T+1/lvl): 15' light; blind (sv). Rev: Darkness — 15' black, not infra.
**Magic Missile** (R:150'): Auto-hit 1d6+1, no sv. +2 missiles/5lvl (3@6th, 5@11th).
**Prot from Evil** (R:self, D:6T): +1 sv, foes –1 atk. Summoned can't melee; breaks if caster attacks.
**Read Languages** (R:self, D:2T): Read any language/code/map. No speech.
**Read Magic** (R:self, D:1T): Read scrolls/books/inscriptions. Once read, permanently readable.
**Shield** (R:self, D:2T): AC 2 vs missiles, AC 4 vs other.
**Sleep** (R:240', D:4d4T): **No save.** 1 creature 4+1HD, or 2d8 HD of ≤4HD. **Weakest first.** Not undead. Helpless=bladed kill. Slap wakes.
**Ventriloquism** (R:60', D:2T): Voice from any point in range.

### L2
**Continual Light** (R:120', D:perm): 30' daylight; blind (sv). Rev: C.Darkness — blocks sight+infra.
**Detect Evil** (R:60', D:2T): Evil intent/objects glow.
**Detect Invisible** (R:10'/lvl, D:6T): See invisible things.
**ESP** (R:60', D:12T): Read thoughts, 1T/direction, +1T isolate one. Any language. Blocked 2'+ rock/thin lead.
**Invisibility** (R:240', D:perm): Invisible until atk/cast. On object: permanent.
**Knock** (R:60', D:1rd): Open stuck/barred/locked/magical door. Opens known secret doors.
**Levitate** (R:self, D:6T+1/lvl): Vertical 20'/rd. Push off surfaces for lateral.
**Locate Object** (R:60'+10'/lvl, D:2T): Direction to object type or specific known. Not creatures.
**Mirror Image** (R:self, D:6T): 1d4 duplicates; any attack destroys one.
**Phantasmal Force** (R:240', D:conc): 20' illusion. Monster: AC9, gone if hit. Atk: sv or affected (death=uncon 1d4T). Scene: gone if touched.
**Web** (R:10', D:48T): 10' cube. Flammable (2rds, 1d6). Break: normal 2d4T, magic STR 4rds, giant 2rds.
**Wizard Lock** (R:10', D:perm): Lock door permanently. Caster passes. Knock or MU 3+lvl higher bypasses.

### L3
**Clairvoyance** (R:60', D:12T): See through creature's eyes, 1T focus, switchable. Blocked 2'+ rock/lead.
**Dispel Magic** (R:120'): End spells in 20' cube. 5% fail/lvl diff if target higher. Not items.
**Fire Ball** (R:240'): 20' radius, 1d6/lvl, sv half.
**Fly** (R:self/touch, D:1d6T+1/lvl): 360'(120') any direction.
**Haste** (R:240', D:3T): ≤24 creatures in 60' dia: 2× move+atks. Not spells.
**Hold Person** (R:120', D:1T/lvl): Paralyse humans, sv. Single –2, group 1d4. No undead/>4+1HD.
**Infravision** (R:self/touch, D:1day): 60' infravision.
**Invis 10'R** (R:120', D:perm): All in 10' invisible, moves with target. Leave/atk=visible for that one.
**Lightning Bolt** (R:180'): 60'×5' bolt, 1d6/lvl, sv half. Bounces off barriers.
**Prot Evil 10'R** (R:10' around, D:12T): As Prot Evil for all in 10'. Breaks if warded melees.
**Prot Normal Missiles** (R:30', D:12T): Immune small non-magic missiles. Not boulders/magic.
**Water Breathing** (R:30', D:1day): Breathe water freely.
