# Exploration
## Dungeon
**Doors**: Secret 1-in-6 to find (10'×10' search). Stuck: STR force (fail=no surprise). Locked: pick/magic. Doors swing shut; spike with iron. Monsters open most doors.
**Listen**: 1-in-6 (some classes better); referee rolls secret. Undead=no noise.
**MV**: Exploring=base rate ft/turn. Familiar=3× rate.
**Turn sequence**: 1)Wandering check 2)Declare actions 3)Resolve; encounters trigger procedure 4)Update time/light/spells/rest.
**Rest**: 1 turn/hr exploring. Penalty: -1 att/dmg til rested 1 turn.
**Traps**: Treasure traps(needle,dart) & room traps(pit,gas). Trigger 2-in-6. Room trap search: 1-in-6 per 10'×10'. Treasure traps: class ability only.
**Wandering monsters**: Check every 2 turns, 1-in-6. Distance 2d6×10'.
**Search**: 1 turn, referee rolls secret, 1 attempt/char/area.
## Wilderness
Distances in yards (3× dungeon ft). Spell areas still ft.
**Flying**: Double overland mi/day. Terrain no effect. Mount HD 3=half-human, 6=human, 12=horse, 24=elephant.
**Forage**: 1-in-6/day while travelling, feeds 1d6. **Hunt**: full day no travel, 1-in-6 encounter.
**Day sequence**: 1)Direction 2)Lost check 3)Wandering check 4)Describe 5)Update time.
**Lost**: Clear/grass 1-in-6. Barren/hills/mtns/woods 2-in-6. Desert/jungle/swamp 3-in-6.
**Overland**: mi/day=MV÷5 (120'=24mi). Broken/desert/forest/hills -33%. Jungle/mtns/swamp -50%. Roads +50%. Forced march +50% then rest 1 day.
**Rest**: 1 day per 6 travel; -1 hit/dmg til rested.
**Visibility**: 3mi open. **Encirclement**: surprised by 3+ may be encircled.
**Wandering**: 1-4×/day, 4d6×10yd (1d4×10 if surprised). City/clear/grass/settled 1-in-6. Aerial/barren/desert/forest/hills 2-in-6. Jungle/mtns/swamp 3-in-6.
## Waterborne
**Lost**: w/navigator 2-in-6. No nav open sea=100%. No nav sight of land=2-in-6. Aquatic monsters usually not surprised.
**Travel**: mi/day=MV÷5. River current ±1d6+6 mi/day.
**Visibility**: Land 24mi clear. Ships 300yd clear, 40yd fog.
**Day sequence**: 1)Course 2)Lost 3)Wind 4)Wandering 5)Describe 6)Update.
**Wandering**: Ocean/river 2-in-6, swamp 3-in-6. Distance 4d6×10yd (1d4×10 surprised).
**Wind(2d6)**: 2=none(no sail). 3-11=normal. 12=gale(3× speed).
**Variable wind(2d6)**: 2=no sail,oars⅓. 3=⅓. 4=½. 5=⅔. 6-8=normal. 9=+⅓. 10=+½. 11=double. 12=triple.
**Storm**: Near gale: seaworthy 10%/unseaworthy 20% taking water→-⅓ speed. Gale(seaworthy): 3× random dir, land=75% wreck. Gale(unseaworthy): 80% sink.
